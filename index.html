About TEDx

What is TEDx?

TEDx is a program of local, self-organized events that bring people together to share a TED-like experience. At TEDxUniversityofAsiaPacific, we gather the brightest minds to share ideas that challenge, inspire, and spark meaningful change.

This event is operated under license from TED and organized entirely by students of the University of Asia Pacific, Dhaka.
